The Owl

In this chapter, we have compiled the literature for the 23 Brazilian owl species, which represent almost one-third of all Neotropical owls. Our main objective here was to review the taxonomy, distribution, ecology, and conservation of each species, both revealing gaps in our knowledge and supplying directions to promote more research on Brazilian owls.

The Owl 1

These are kind of cool!

Owls are birds from the order Strigiformes, which includes over 200 species of mostly solitary and nocturnal birds of prey typified by an upright stance, a large, broad head, binocular vision, binaural hearing, sharp talons , and feathers adapted for silent flight.
